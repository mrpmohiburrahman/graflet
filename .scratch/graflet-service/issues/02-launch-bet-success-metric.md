# Set the launch bet, success metric, target user, and kill criterion

Type: grilling
Status: resolved
Mode: HITL
Blocked by: —

## Question

What bet is this launch - a weekend validation experiment (measure demand, no revenue expected) or a product meant to charge and stay fresh? Define what 'v1 done' means (a single end-to-end path works: pick shadcn -> email -> download a valid bundle) AND what launch success looks like in numbers (signups? completed downloads? paying users?), the kill/keep-going criterion after N weeks, and who the v1 target user is (humans steering AI coding agents vs devs wanting an offline docs graph). This root bet calibrates how much to invest in pricing, accounts, and freshness before shipping.

---

**Why session-sized / why this type:** The founder's ambition level is the root bet that frames every money/identity/scope call and sets the finish line; without it the MVP drifts forever.

## Answer

Experiment-first. v1 is fully FREE but SIGNUP-GATED (must sign up to download). KPI / kill-keep = 100 signups in 3 weeks. After that milestone, paywall a SUBSET of bundles. Target user = developers working with AI coding agents who want a pre-built docs knowledge graph without burning the tokens/time to build it themselves.
