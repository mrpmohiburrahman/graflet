# Choose the product name, brand tone, and domain

Type: grilling
Status: open
Mode: HITL
Blocked by: 14

## Question

What is the product name, domain, and brand tone? Does the name lead on the graph (map/atlas/graph), on docs-to-download, or on for-LLMs? Utilitarian dev-tool voice or playful? Check domain availability for the top 2-3 candidates, pick one that fits the positioning wedge, and register it (the purchase is folded into this - no separate buy-domain ticket unless a collision forces re-naming).

---

**Why session-sized / why this type:** Name/brand/domain is pure founder identity and should follow the positioning wedge so the name reinforces the pitch; the domain registration is a trivial follow-on of this decision.
