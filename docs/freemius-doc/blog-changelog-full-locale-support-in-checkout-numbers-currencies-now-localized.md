[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Full Locale Support in Checkout: Numbers & Currencies Now Localized

Quite some time ago, we introduced [localization for our Checkout](blog-changelog-freemius-checkout-translations-out-of-beta.md), releasing it in five languages other than English. Our goal at Freemius is simple when it comes to the Checkout: provide the most optimized experience for buyers.

With that in mind, this week we’re rolling out an enhancement to the existing localization. Now, all numbers and currencies displayed in the Checkout will follow the selected locale or language.

[![Freemius Checkout currencies localization](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/freemius-checkout-cart-number-localization-1024x609.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/freemius-checkout-cart-number-localization.png)

This update applies to both the cart section and the final review step shown before payment.

[![Freemius Checkout Review phase localization with currencies and numbers](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/freemius-checkout-review-number-localization-1024x651.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/freemius-checkout-review-number-localization.png)

We hope this improvement offers a more seamless and familiar experience to your buyers, helping you drive more conversions.

Buyers can always choose their preferred language directly from the UI.

[![Freemius Language Selector UI](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/freemius-language-selector-ui-1024x673.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/freemius-language-selector-ui.png)

If you’re looking to automatically pre-select the language based on the buyer’s location, you can refer to [our documentation](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-freemius-checkout-buy-button.md#language) for implementation details.
