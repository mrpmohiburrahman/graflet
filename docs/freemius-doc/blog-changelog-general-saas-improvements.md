[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / General SaaS improvements

We have various small and medium tweaks to our system to make it more useful for SaaS makers. The changes were made across the Developer Dashboard, the backend, and the Customer Portal. Please find the details below.

### Decluttering the Developer Dashboard

We have removed or enhanced many things that do not pertain to SaaS products.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/freemius-saas-plans-page-1024x663.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/freemius-saas-plans-page.png)

We improved the Plans configuration.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/freemius-saas-checkout-customization-1024x475.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/freemius-saas-checkout-customization.png)

The Customization section was also simplified.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/freemius-saas-settings-page-1024x781.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/freemius-saas-settings-page.png)

The Settings page also got a revamp.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/freemius-setup-list-enhancements-1024x312.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/freemius-setup-list-enhancements.png)

The setup page also updated some inline documentation.

### Removed License related information from the Customer Portal

Recently we [pushed a change](blog-changelog-freemius-for-saas-ability-to-expose-license-keys-to-buyers.md) to allow SaaS products to decide whether to expose licenses to the users.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/freemius-user-dashboard-hidden-license-for-saas-1024x434.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/freemius-user-dashboard-hidden-license-for-saas.png)

Following up, we’ve updated the Customer Portal to show or hide any license-related information depending on the configuration.
