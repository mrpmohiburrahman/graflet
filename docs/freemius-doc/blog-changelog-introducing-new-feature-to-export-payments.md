[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Introducing new feature to export Payments

Following up on our [data liberation project](blog-changelog-enhancements-to-the-users-export-from-the-developer-dashboard.md), we are glad to announce the immediate availability of payment exports from the [Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.freemius.com/#!/live/new-product/).

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/payments-export-1024x877.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/payments-export.png)

Go to the “Payments” page and you will see the “Download” button. You can choose to download all payments or just the ones showing up in the UI.

While Freemius offers [intensive reports and analytics tools](blog-release-notes-real-time-analytics-and-multi-store-dashboard.md), the export feature is beneficial for understanding the data and doing your custom reporting. Please stay tuned while we introduce such export options for more assets.
