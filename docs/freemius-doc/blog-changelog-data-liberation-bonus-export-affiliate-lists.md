[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Data Liberation Bonus: Export affiliate lists

Just as we were about to wrap up our [data liberation project](blog-freemius-release-notes-january-2025.md), we received an exciting request from our makers that we couldn’t ignore. In addition to [exporting affiliate payments](blog-changelog-new-feature-affiliate-payouts-download.md), they asked for the ability to export the list of affiliates as well.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/freemius-affiliates-export-1024x596.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/freemius-affiliates-export.png)

We’re thrilled to announce the immediate availability of this feature! Simply navigate to the **“Affiliates”** tab under the **“Affiliate Program”** page, where you’ll find a **Download** button. This allows you to export all your affiliates along with details such as their name, email, CTR, earnings, and more.

If you haven’t already, be sure to explore our [Affiliate Platform](help-documentation-affiliate-platform.md) feature to grow your business.
