[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Customer Portal Revamped: EU Accessibility Compliant and Soft Rebranded

[![Order History page of Freemius Customer Portal](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/freemius-customer-portal-order-history-1024x528.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/freemius-customer-portal-order-history.png)

We recently released a major update to our [Checkout](blog-changelog-freemius-checkout-is-now-eu-accessibility-compliant.md), making it fully compliant with the [upcoming EU accessibility law](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/web-accessibility). Today, we’re excited to share that the [**Freemius Customer Portal**](help-documentation-users-account-management.md) is now also compliant.

[![Login page of Freemius Customer Portal](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/customer-portal-login-raw-1024x721.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/customer-portal-login-raw.png)

The Customer Portal now adheres to the [WAI accessibility guidelines](https://www.w3.org/WAI/ARIA/apg/patterns/), covering both mandatory requirements and best practices. We’ve ensured proper contrast ratios, clear focus states, and fully accessible interactive elements for all users.

[![Websites page from Freemius Customer Portal](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/customer-portal-website-install-1024x439.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/customer-portal-website-install.png)

This update ensures your buyers can seamlessly access their [**order history and license details**](help-documentation-users-account-management-orders-history.md), regardless of their device or assistive technology. If you’re [embedding the Customer Portal](https://github.com/Freemius/freemius-users-dashboard) on your own website, this also helps you remain compliant with the EU accessibility law coming into effect on **June 28th, 2025**.

### Soft Rebranding with Accessibility in Mind

Alongside these changes, we’ve introduced a **soft rebranding** of the Customer Portal using our new color palette. These updates have been implemented carefully to avoid breaking any of your existing custom CSS overrides.

[![Affiliate page - Freemius Customer Portal](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/customer-portal-affiliate-1024x433.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/customer-portal-affiliate.png)

This rebranding is just the beginning. Stay tuned as we continue modernizing the Customer Portal with more improvements in the coming months.
