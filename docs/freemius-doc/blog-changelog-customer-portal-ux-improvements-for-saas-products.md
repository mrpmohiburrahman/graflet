[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Customer Portal UX Improvements for SaaS Products

We are deploying a small update to our [Customer Portal](help-documentation-users-account-management.md) to make it better suited for [SaaS products](help-documentation-saas.md).

[![SaaS customer portal](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/customer-portal-subscription-sidepane-1024x389.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/customer-portal-subscription-sidepane.png)

1. The default landing page will now be **Subscriptions** instead of **Invoices**, so buyers can immediately see their active plans.
2. The subscription detail side pane will no longer show license details, since they are not relevant to buyers and are used internally by SaaS products to [maintain entitlements](help-documentation-saas-saas-integration.md#saving-purchase-information).
