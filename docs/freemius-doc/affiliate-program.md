## How Does It Work?

- Your affiliation with the Seller remains active for 12 months from the date of the Seller’s account registration.
- If the Seller does not make a sale within 90 days of account registration, your affiliation to the Seller is disconnected and future sales are treated as if you never referred the Seller to Freemius. We figured 3 months is pretty reasonable for everyone.
- The maximum affiliate payout per referral is $1,000.
