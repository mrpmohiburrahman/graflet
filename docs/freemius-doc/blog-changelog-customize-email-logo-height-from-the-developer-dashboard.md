[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Customize Email Logo Height From the Developer Dashboard

This week, we are rolling out a new feature in our [Email Style Customization](https://freemius.com/help/documentation/emails/email-style-customization/). This came in as a request from one of our makers, so we are excited to make it available. Up until now, makers could set a logo for their emails, but the logo size was capped at a maximum height of 50px.

[![Email Style Customization interface showing the new logo height setting](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/email-style-customization-logo-height-1024x480.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/07/email-style-customization-logo-height.png)

With today’s deployment, you can now adjust the height of the email logo to a specific value of your choice. We hope this gives you more control over your email design and helps your emails better reflect your brand. If you have any feedback, please feel free to contact our support team from the Developer Dashboard.
