[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Fixed alignment issues with the sharable cards

Freemius has [powerful marketing](help-documentation-marketing-automation.md) automation with [Ratings & Reviews](help-documentation-marketing-automation-reviews.md). Apart from getting reviews, you can also use the Developer Dashboard to generate and share card-like images.

[![Getting social sharable cards of reviews from Freemius Developer Dashboard](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/getting-sharable-card-from-freemius-dashboard-1024x431.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/getting-sharable-card-from-freemius-dashboard.png)

We noticed an issue where the review title could sometimes get misaligned. We have pushed a fix for the same.

[![A sample review card](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/freemius-review-sharable-card-1024x538.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/freemius-review-sharable-card.png)

Now the images generated look properly aligned.
