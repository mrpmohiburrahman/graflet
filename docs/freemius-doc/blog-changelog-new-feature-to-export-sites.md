[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / New Feature to Export Sites

As part of our ongoing [Data Liberation project](blog-freemius-release-notes-october.md), we’ve added export functionality for **Sites**.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/sites-export-freemius-developer-dashboard-1024x705.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/sites-export-freemius-developer-dashboard.png)

To get started, navigate to the **Sites** page under your Developer Dashboard and click **Download** in the top-right corner.

We’re nearing the finish line for this project—stay tuned for more updates!
