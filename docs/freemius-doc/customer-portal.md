Customers can change payment methods by logging in to your website, heading to the Renewals & Billing tab, and making the switch. Whether they need to update their credit card details or change from PayPal to a credit card (or the other way around), it’s simple and quick.

Customers who want to cancel or upgrade their subscription can simply log in and go to the Licenses tab. Freemius takes care of proration, trials, and renewals automatically, removing any unnecessary hassle from their shoulders.

For downloadable software like desktop apps and WordPress plugins, users may need to access their downloads, upgrade a license, or even whitelist a site for license activation. All you need to do is point them to the Customer Portal and let them know they can manage all of the above quickly and easily.

Customers can simply access their Customer Portal, update the VAT ID in their profile, and head to the Order History tab to generate a new EU VAT invoice.
