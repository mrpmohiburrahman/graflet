# Affiliate Platform

Freemius includes a fully-featured Affiliate Platform that you can use for free, right out of the box when selling with Freemius, to run your own Affiliate Program.

Setting up an Affiliate Program can be a powerful, long-term investment that will help drive more sales, boost your SEO, and increase brand awareness. However, if not planned or executed correctly, it can become a significant drain on time and resources. If you are a solo maker just starting your software business journey, carefully weigh your options before diving into the world of affiliate marketing.

tip

We highly recommend reading [The Ultimate Guide To An Affiliate Program](blog-affiliate-program-wordpress-plugins-themes.md) before setting up your program. While it is aimed at WordPress, the principles are highly relevant to any software maker.
