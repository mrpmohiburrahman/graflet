[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Enhancements to iDEAL Support in Freemius Checkout

Recently, we released [iDEAL support for our Checkout](blog-changelog-checkout-related-enhancements-for-the-developer-dashboard.md) to ease payments from buyers in the Netherlands.

[![Freemius Checkout Update Flow with iDEAL](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/payment-method-update-with-ideal-1024x768.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/payment-method-update-with-ideal.png)

This week, we’re continuing to strengthen that support with a few enhancements and bug fixes:

- Fixed an edge case issue related to license renewals via iDEAL.
- Payment method updates now work seamlessly both *to* and *from* iDEAL.
- Improved error handling for broken or interrupted payment flows.

To enable iDEAL as a payment method in your Checkout, simply configure your product pricing in EUR. Once set, buyers from the Netherlands will automatically see iDEAL as one of the supported payment methods.
