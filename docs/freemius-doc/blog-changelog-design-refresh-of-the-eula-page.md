[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Design refresh of the EULA page

We have updated the design of Freemius products’ EULA page to feature a modern, user-friendly appearance.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/freemius-product-eula-1024x517.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/freemius-product-eula.png)

[Example EULA page of a Freemius product](https://freemius.com/product/311/freemius-awesome-plugin-pro/legal/eula/)

You can access the End User License Agreement (EULA) for your product at the following URL:

- https://freemius.com/product/`<productID>`/`<productSlug>`/legal/eula/

Please replace `<productID>` and `<slug>` with the relevant values, which can be found under the “Settings” page of your product in the [Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.freemius.com/).

The EULA page is used during checkout, by affiliates, and in other related processes.
