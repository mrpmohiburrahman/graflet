## FAQ

### Should I arrive with my WordCamp badge?

Yes, because spots are limited and it’s an invite-only event, we will check for both your badge and your ticket at the entrance. This is a social event and wearing your badge will make it easier for attendees to recognize or get to know you.

### I want to bring a friend who didn’t sign up for the meetup

The event is for product owners, founders, and partners. However, if you want to bring a team member or +1, email [\[email protected\]](https://freemius.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection) a day before the event and let us know. If we have open spots, we'll happily add them to the list.

### I didn’t receive a confirmation email

If you can’t find an email from us, please contact [\[email protected\]](https://freemius.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection) a day before the event and we will be happy to check for you.

### I can’t come to the event

If you’re unable to attend the event, we kindly ask that you inform us at [\[email protected\]](https://freemius.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection). Your consideration is greatly appreciated as there are eager individuals on our waiting list who will be happy to hear we’ve found a spot for them.

### I received an email saying that I’m on the waiting list

Unfortunately, spots are limited, but this is not the final word just yet! Often, people can’t make it to the meetup so make sure to check you inbox — perhaps you'll find a ticket with your name on it!

### Where is the event?

The event is taking place at Otium Rooftop, which is an 8-minute walk from the WordCamp venue.
