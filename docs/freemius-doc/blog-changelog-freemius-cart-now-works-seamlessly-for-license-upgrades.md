[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Freemius Cart Now Works Seamlessly for License Upgrades

Our [cart mechanism](help-documentation-marketing-automation-cart-abandonment-recovery.md) now fully supports the license upgrade flow. A license upgrade flow occurs when your buyers attempt to upgrade their plan—whether through the [Customer Portal](help-documentation-users-account-management.md), the [WordPress SDK](help-documentation-wordpress-sdk.md), or a [custom integration](help-documentation-saas-saas-integration.md#handling-license-or-subscription-upgrades).

Previously, our cart system had limited support for this flow. With this update, it now functions seamlessly for all upgrade scenarios.

[![Freemius Cart Example](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/freemius-cart-997x1024.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/freemius-cart.png)

Now, when your buyers initiate a plan upgrade but abandon the process midway, Freemius’ cart recovery process will automatically trigger to help recover the transaction—boosting your conversion rate and revenue.
