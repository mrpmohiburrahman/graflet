Discover the powerful features of the Freemius Checkout, designed to support your business needs. These features enhance the payment experience for your customers, improve conversion rates, and streamline your sales process, including:

- [Proration](help-documentation-checkout-features-proration.md): Manage subscription changes efficiently with related benefits.
- [Automatic Discounts](help-documentation-checkout-features-automatic-discounts-in-checkout.md): Allow customers to see their savings during checkout.
- [Billing Cycle Selector](help-documentation-checkout-features-billing-cycle-selector-ui.md): Provide flexible billing options to your customers.
- [Social Proofing](help-documentation-checkout-features-social-proofing-ui.md): Leverage social proof to boost conversions.
- [Upsell Toggles](help-documentation-checkout-features-upsell-toggles.md): Increase your average order value.
- [Coupons](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-coupon-discount.md): Offer discounts and promotions during checkout.
- [Trials](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-set-up-trials.md): Let customers try your product before purchasing.
- [Multi-Currency Pricing](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-multi-currency.md): Sell in multiple currencies to reach a global audience.
- [GDPR Compliance](help-documentation-checkout-features-gdpr.md): Ensure your checkout follows GDPR regulations.
- [User Token](help-documentation-checkout-features-pre-populate-user-info.md): Prepopulate user information to streamline checkout.
- [Tracking & Analytics](help-documentation-checkout-features-tracking-analytics.md): Monitor and analyze checkout performance.
