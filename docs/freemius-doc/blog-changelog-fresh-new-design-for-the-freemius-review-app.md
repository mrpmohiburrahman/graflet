[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Fresh New Design for the Freemius Review App

Our review app just received a modern makeover. Freemius automatically helps you collect reviews from your buyers, giving you an easy way to build social proof without extra effort.

The flow begins with an automated email that redirects buyers to the review app.

[![Freemius review app](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/freemius-review-app-1024x893.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/freemius-review-app.png)

Until now, the app’s interface felt a bit outdated. With today’s deployment, we’re introducing a refreshed design aligned with our [ongoing rebranding work](blog-freemius-rebrand-website-developer-dashboard-redesign.md). The new look brings a cleaner, more modern experience that leaves a stronger impression on your buyers.

You can learn more about the review collection system in our [documentation](help-documentation-marketing-automation-reviews.md).
