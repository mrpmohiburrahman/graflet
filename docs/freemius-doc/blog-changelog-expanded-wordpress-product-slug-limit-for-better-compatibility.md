[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Expanded WordPress Product Slug Limit for Better Compatibility

It came to our attention that the previous `64`-character limit for a [WordPress product’s slug](help-documentation-wordpress.md) was too restrictive. Since the WordPress.org repository allows up to `100` characters, some makers found it difficult to align their product details when syncing with the official repo.

[![Long slug while creating product in Freemius](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/long-slug-in-wp-product-freemius-dashboard-1024x439.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/long-slug-in-wp-product-freemius-dashboard.png)

To ensure better compatibility and smoother onboarding for products originating from WordPress.org, we’ve increased our slug limit to `100` characters. This update applies both to the new product creation form and the Settings page of existing products.
