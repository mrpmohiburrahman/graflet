[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / White labelling licenses from the Developer Dashboard

Owners can flag a license as “White Labeled” to easily hide confidential information about both their account and license. Up until now, this feature was only available on our [User Dashboard](https://users.freemius.com).

Thanks to a recent update, it is now possible for developers to do the same on the [Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.freemius.com).

[![Is White Label Toggling Developer Dashboard](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/freemius-is-white-label-developer-dashboard.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/freemius-is-white-label-developer-dashboard.png)

Please go to the Developer Dashboard &gt; Licenses and toggle the switch under the newly introduced column to **Is White-labeled**. This feature is also a part of our [Beta Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard-beta.freemius.com).

Read more about it from our [documentation](help-documentation-users-account-management-license-security.md#white_label_mode).
