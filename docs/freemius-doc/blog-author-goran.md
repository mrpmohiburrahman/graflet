Goran is a seasoned professional in the B2B SaaS industry with over 10 years of experience in content, SEO, and growth marketing. With a focus on data-driven insights, customer engagement, and optimized content, Goran has led teams at various B2B content marketing agencies, including Animalz and Megawatt.
