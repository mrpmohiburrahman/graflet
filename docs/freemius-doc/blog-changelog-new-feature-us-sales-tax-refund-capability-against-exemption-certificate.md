[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / New feature: US Sales tax refund capability against exemption certificate

We are glad to announce the immediate availability of a new feature our partners have requested. If you have buyers from the US with sales tax exemption, you can now issue a full tax refund to the buyer from your Developer Dashboard.

[![Accessing refund for a payment from Freemius Developer Dashboard](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/accessing-refund-freemius-dashboard-payments-page-1024x614.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/accessing-refund-freemius-dashboard-payments-page.png)

Go to the “Payments” page of the Developer Dashboard and click the Refund button next to the payment.

[![Refunding US Sales Tax against exemption certificate in Freemius Developer Dashboard](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/us-sales-tax-refund-freemius-developer-dashboard-877x1024.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/us-sales-tax-refund-freemius-developer-dashboard.png)

Select the “Tax refund of $..” option and provide a valid Tax exemption certificate number. Please ask your buyer to give this number to you. Then click the “Yes – Refund” button after accepting the disclaimer and you will be all set.

Like VAT refunds, [two fresh invoices will be created](blog-changelog-improvements-to-the-vat-refund-feature.md), one as a full refund for the original transaction and another without the tax, containing the tax certificate number. Similarly, if the payment is associated with a subscription, the subscription will be automatically adjusted not to charge sales tax for future renewals.
