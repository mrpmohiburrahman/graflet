## Features and benefits

![Affiliate management](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Affiliate-management-1.png)

### Affiliate management

Handle affiliates and affiliate terms in one simple dashboard.

![Affiliate portal](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Affiliate-portal-2.png)

### Affiliate portal

Affiliates can easily access program terms, retrieve their referral URLs, track referrals and visits, and view payout history along with their expected balance — all in one place directly from the [Customer Portal](customer-portal.md).

![One-click activation](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/One-click-activation-2.png)

### One-click activation

Already have a Freemius account with a product added? You can activate your affiliate program with the flip of a switch.

![Subscription renewal commissions](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Subscription-renewal-commissions-2.png)

### Subscription renewal commissions

Choose whether affiliates earn commissions on renewals, and set limits, such as only rewarding for the first 12 months of a subscription.

![Flexible affiliate application approval](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Flexible-affiliate-application-approval-1.png)

### Flexible affiliate application approval

Choose between manual moderation, auto-approving customers, or allowing any user — free or paid — to automatically join your program.

![Flexible affiliate restrictions](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Flexible-affiliate-restrictions-1.png)

### Flexible affiliate restrictions

Choose to restrict your affiliate program to invitation-only or open it up for anyone to join.

![Freemium conversion tracking](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Freemium-conversion-tracking-2.png)

### Freemium conversion tracking

Affiliate referrals to free versions of your product can be tracked and credited when users upgrade to premium plans.

![Flexible commission options](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Flexible-commission-options-2.png)

### Flexible commission options

Choose to pay referral commissions as a percentage or a fixed amount, with options for renewal or time-based tracking.

![Real-time stats](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Real-time-stats-1.png)

### Real-time stats

See live stats about CTR, earnings, referrals, and visits.

![Police promotion channels](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Police-promotion-channels-1.png)

### Police promotion channels

Control how and where your marketing and promotions are shared, such as through email, mobile, social media, or websites to reach relevant audiences.

![Flexible tracking expiration](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Flexible-tracking-expiration-2.png)

### Flexible tracking expiration

Set the cookie expiration time according to your preferences for affiliate terms.

![Affiliate coupon codes](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Affiliate-coupon-codes-2.png)

### Affiliate coupon codes

Coupon-based referral tracking for affiliates to drive YouTube and podcast promotions without needing referral links.

![Custom affiliate terms](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Custom-affiliate-terms-2.png)

### Custom affiliate terms

Set custom terms to create unique partnerships with selected affiliates.

![Lifetime commissions](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Lifetime-commissions-1.png)

### Lifetime commissions

Reward super affiliates with ongoing commissions for every future sale from the customers they refer.

![Track payouts](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Track-payouts-2.png)

### Track payouts

Monitor when affiliates are due for payment and keep track of their payout status.

![Control the referral period <br>(aka cookie expiration)](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Control-the-referral-period-aka-cookie-expiration-1.png)

### Control the referral period (aka cookie expiration)

Set custom cookie durations to any number of days or choose 'session-based' tracking, rewarding affiliates only for referrals completed within the same browser session after an affiliate link is clicked.

![Commission eligibility by customer type](https://v3.freemius.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Commission-eligibility-by-customer-type-1.png)

### Commission eligibility by customer type

Decide whether affiliates earn commissions for all customers, only first-time buyers, or brand-new users who have never interacted with your product.
