You can easily customize the affiliation terms for each affiliate.

First, scroll to the bottom of the affiliate’s details and click the **Customize affiliation terms** switch:

If you click it, all the terms of the program will show up and you will be able to customize each detail, including the affiliate’s commission, cookie expiration period, whether or not the affiliate should be rewarded for subscriptions, and more.
