[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Bug Fix: Reverse Invoices Billing Address

We recently introduced the [*Reverse Invoice*](blog-changelog-new-feature-reverse-invoices-for-your-payouts.md) feature, where Freemius generates the payout invoice on your behalf.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/freemius-reverse-invoice-1024x764.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/freemius-reverse-invoice.png)

We identified a minor bug where, in some cases, the business name on the invoice wasn’t pulled from your bank wire configuration. This issue has now been fixed.
