Freemius recognizes the tremendous value and potential of open-source software in driving innovation, collaboration, and accessibility in the WordPress world. However, we also understand that open-source development comes with its own set of challenges, ranging from sustainability and funding to governance and community management.

Our goal is to shed light on these challenges and provide practical guidance for overcoming them to ensure the long-term success and viability of open-source projects.

In this category, you'll find a diverse range of topics covering various challenges in open-source development, from attracting contributors and maintaining project momentum to managing community dynamics and addressing security vulnerabilities. Our articles offer insights, case studies, and best practices to help developers and project maintainers navigate these challenges and build thriving open-source communities.

For example, in our article Contributing to WordPress: The Good, the Bad & the Polyglot, we look at why the WordPress community is so passionate about keeping the software in an open-source state despite the challenges posed by projects like Five for the Future. Additionally, our founder and CEO Vova Feldman presents an in-depth guide on how to legally protect your GPL plugin business against trolls.

Empower yourself to build thriving, sustainable, and impactful open-source projects that benefit users and communities around the world.
