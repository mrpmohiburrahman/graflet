[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Fixed Billing Country Display in the Customer Portal

We identified an issue in our [Customer Portal](help-documentation-users-account-management.md) where the country dropdown was not correctly reflecting the user’s billing country.

[![Customer Portal billing form](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/customer-portal-billing-form-1024x879.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/customer-portal-billing-form.png)

This has now been fixed, and the dropdown will consistently show the correct billing country for the user.
