[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Freemius Checkout Now Fully Supports Hrvatski

Last week we released [beta support](blog-changelog-freemius-checkout-updates-license-based-billing-defaults-and-croatian-language-support.md) for the Hrvatski language in Croatia. This week, with help from our maker community, we are glad to release it out of beta.

[![Checkout Now Fully Supports Hrvatski](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/croatian-language-out-of-beta-1024x982.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/croatian-language-out-of-beta.png)

This means you can now simply use the `auto` value for the [language](help-documentation-checkout-integration-freemius-checkout-buy-button.md#language%20%7C%20locale) parameter, and buyers coming from Croatia will see the Checkout in their language.

If you would like us to translate our Checkout into your own language, please feel free to get in touch with our support team and we can work it out.
