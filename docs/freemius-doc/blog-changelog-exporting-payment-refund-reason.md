[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Exporting payment refund reason

Freemius allows you to issue full or partial [refunds](help-faq-all.md#can_i_refund_my_customers) to your customers. When issuing a refund, you can also add a **Refund reason** for your team members to see.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/freemius-payment-refund-dialog-710x1024.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/freemius-payment-refund-dialog.png)

Now, when exporting [payments](blog-changelog-introducing-new-feature-to-export-payments.md), if you select the “Refunds” filter from the UI, the exported CSV will include the refund reason.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/payment-refund-reason-1024x546.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/payment-refund-reason.png)

This update is designed to help our makers perform custom analytics with all the necessary information.
