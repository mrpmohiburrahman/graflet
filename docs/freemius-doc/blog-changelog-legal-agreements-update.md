We’ve introduced **Terms & Conditions of Sale**, a legal agreement for SaaS products, which is now part of the checkout experience.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/saas-tcs-1024x555.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/saas-tcs.png)

If you’re using [Freemius integration with your SaaS products](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-saas-integration.md), the **Terms & Conditions of Sale** page is available at the following link:

```
https://freemius.com/product/<productID>/<productSlug>/legal/terms-of-sale
```

The `productID` and `productSlug` can be found on the **Settings** page in the [Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.freemius.com/).
