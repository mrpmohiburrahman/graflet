[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Analytics Performance Improvements for Large Products

We noticed an issue in our [Analytics pages](help-documentation-analytics.md) where, for some large products, requests could time out due to high data volume. After identifying the root cause, we have deployed the first round of performance optimizations to improve response times and stability.

[![Freemius Analytics Page](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/freemius-analytics-page-1024x626.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/freemius-analytics-page.png)

If you continue to experience any slowdown on these pages, please reach out to our support team and let us know.
