[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Fixed Developer team member permission issue when accessing custom webhook and Mailchimp

It came to our attention that the [custom webhook](help-documentation-marketing-automation-events-webhooks.md) and [Mailchimp integration](help-documentation-integrations-mailchimp-integration.md) pages from the Developer Dashboard were blocking ‘write’ access to team members with the role of ‘Developer’. In this deployment, we’ve fixed the issue.

Now team members with the ‘Developer’ role can modify webhooks and Mailchimp integration.
