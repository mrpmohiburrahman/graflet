[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / New UI for the New Commission Rate

Not too long ago, we revamped our [pricing model](pricing.md) to better serve [SaaS and App](freemius-for-saas.md) makers. Today, we’re rolling out a fresh new UI in the [Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.freemius.com/) to reflect those changes.

[![Freemius Earnings page Payouts Information UI](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/refreshed-earnings-page-payout-info-1024x560.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/refreshed-earnings-page-payout-info.png)

When you visit the [**Earnings**](https://dashboard.freemius.com/#!/earnings/) page, you’ll now see a new graph that clearly explains the different commission rates Freemius applies based on product type.

These updated commission rates have already been factored into your earnings calculations since the announcement. This UI update is aimed at making things more transparent.

Additionally, the **Payouts** information section is now collapsible. If you choose to collapse it, your browser will remember the preference—freeing up vertical space and allowing you to instantly focus on your earnings when opening the page.

If you have any questions about the new pricing, feel free to reach out via support.
