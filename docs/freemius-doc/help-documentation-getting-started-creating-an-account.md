The first step to selling your software product with Freemius is to [create an account](https://dashboard.freemius.com/register/) through the [Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.freemius.com/register/). It's **completely free** to get started!

After creating an account, the next step is to integrate your product into Freemius.
