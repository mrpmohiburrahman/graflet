[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Easier Access to Customer & Legal Links from Product Settings

We have added a new tab to the Product Settings page called **Useful Links**. This tab brings together several self-service tools—such as the [Customer Portal](help-documentation-users-account-management.md) and the [License Recovery Tool](help-documentation-wordpress-license-recovery-tool.md)—that you can easily share with your customers. In addition, it also lists all legal pages that Freemius generates for your product as your [MoR](merchant-of-record.md).

[![Useful links in the Freemius Developer Dashboard](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/useful-links-developer-dashboard-1024x529.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/useful-links-developer-dashboard.png)

You can access this section from **Settings → Useful Links**.

By grouping these resources in a single place, this update makes it easier to discover and share some of the built-in tools Freemius provides to help with customer support and product management.
