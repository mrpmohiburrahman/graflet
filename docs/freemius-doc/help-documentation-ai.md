Freemius AI tools help you easily integrate Freemius into your products through AI coding assistants, no-code (or vibe-code) platforms, and more.

We provide instructions and code snippets to AI agents so they understand how to integrate Freemius into your product, use its various features, and leverage it to monetize your application.

For example, with a simple prompt like:

prompt

```md
Integrate Freemius into my application and gate premium features behind a paywall.
```

your AI agent can integrate the [Freemius Checkout and licensing system](help-documentation-saas-saas-integration.md) into your product and start monetizing your application in minutes.
