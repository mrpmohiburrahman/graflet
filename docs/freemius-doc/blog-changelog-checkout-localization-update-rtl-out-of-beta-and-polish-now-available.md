[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Checkout Localization Update: RTL Out of Beta and Polish Now Available

This week, we are rolling out the following changes to the Freemius Checkout.

## RTL Out of Beta

A few weeks ago, we rolled out [RTL support for our Checkout in beta](blog-changelog-rtl-language-support-comes-to-freemius-checkout.md). After several rounds of feedback and iteration, we are glad to announce that RTL and Hebrew language support is now officially out of beta and production-ready.

[![Checkout RTL support out of beta](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/freemius-checkout-rtl-support-1024x633.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/freemius-checkout-rtl-support.png)

This means that if you load the Checkout with the `language: 'auto'` parameter, it will automatically load in Hebrew for customers visiting from Israel. You can read more about it in our [documentation](help-documentation-checkout-features-local-languages-currencies.md).

## Polish Support

In addition, the Polish language is also ready for production and is available under the language code `pl`.

[![Checkout Polish language support](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/freemius-checkout-polish-language-1024x616.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/freemius-checkout-polish-language.png)

## Faster Language Loading

We have also improved how languages are loaded in the Checkout. Customers should now see the selected language almost instantly, instead of waiting a few hundred milliseconds as before.

[![Freemius Checkout language selector](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/freemius-checkout-language-selector.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/freemius-checkout-language-selector.png)

We hope these changes continue to bring Freemius closer to your [local market](help-documentation-checkout-features-local-languages-currencies.md).

## Fixed Renewal Discount Visibility

Additionally, we noticed that for [WordPress](help-documentation-wordpress.md) and [Template](help-documentation-selling-templates.md) products, the [renewal discount](help-documentation-selling-templates-templates-plans-pricing.md#renewals-discount) was not always surfaced in the UI.

[![Checkout renewal discount line](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/freemius-checkout-renewal-discount-line-1024x472.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/freemius-checkout-renewal-discount-line.png)

This has been fixed as well.
