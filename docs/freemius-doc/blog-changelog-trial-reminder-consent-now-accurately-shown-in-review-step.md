[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Trial Reminder Consent Now Accurately Shown in Review Step

Freemius [Checkout](help-documentation-checkout.md) shows a checkbox for [trial purchases](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-set-up-trials.md) that allows buyers to opt in to receiving a reminder email before the trial ends. This reminder helps buyers stay informed before an upcoming charge and gives them time to decide whether to continue or cancel. The same email also plays an important role in reducing disputes and chargebacks.

[![Freemius checkout with trial reminder checkbox](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/checkout-trial-reminder-checkbox-1024x761.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/checkout-trial-reminder-checkbox.png)

However, we noticed that the buyer’s selection was not being properly reflected in the **Review** step. The UI always indicated that a reminder email would be sent, even when the buyer had explicitly unchecked the checkbox.

[![Trial reminder in the review step](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/checkout-trial-reminder-review-1024x816.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/checkout-trial-reminder-review.png)

This has now been fixed. The review step will correctly reflect the buyer’s choice and clearly indicate whether a trial reminder email will be sent.
