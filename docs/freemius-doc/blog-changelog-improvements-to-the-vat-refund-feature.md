[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Improvements to the VAT Refund feature

We have enhanced the newly introduced [VAT Refund feature](blog-changelog-new-feature-to-allow-vat-refunds.md). After making the refund, in the system, we mark the original payment as fully refunded, and then we create a new payment without the VAT while giving a refund to the customer.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/payments-filter-freemius-developer-dashboard-1024x475.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/payments-filter-freemius-developer-dashboard.png)

Previously you had to click on the “Refunds” or “All” filter to see all three transactions.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/vat-refund-transactions-freemius-developer-dashboard-1024x538.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/vat-refund-transactions-freemius-developer-dashboard.png)

But now, the three transactions will appear under the “Not Refunded” filter right out of the box.
