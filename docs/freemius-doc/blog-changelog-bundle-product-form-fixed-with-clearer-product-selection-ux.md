[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Bundle Product Form Fixed With Clearer Product Selection UX

We identified a regression in the [bundle](help-documentation-wordpress-selling-bundles-and-memberships.md) flow where the new product form could not render the product type field in the UI when adding a product to a bundle. This has now been fixed.

[![New bundled product form](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/new-bundle-product-form-1024x761.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/new-bundle-product-form.png)

In addition, we have improved the UX of the dialog used while adding products to a bundle to make the flow clearer.

[![New bundled product dialog](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/new-bundle-product-dialog-1024x673.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/new-bundle-product-dialog.png)

It is now more obvious how to add an existing product to the bundle or create a new product within the bundle flow.
