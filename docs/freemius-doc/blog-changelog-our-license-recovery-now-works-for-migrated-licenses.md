[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Our License Recovery now Works for Migrated Licenses

At Freemius, we’ve built a robust [license recovery mechanism](help-documentation-marketing-automation-transactional-emails.md#marketing-automation-emails) that helps makers recover lost sales and subscriptions. Whenever a renewal fails or a subscription gets cancelled, our system automatically sends a sequence of reminder and renewal emails to bring the customer back.

[![Freemius License Renewal Email](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/license-expiration-renewal-reminder-1024x783.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/license-expiration-renewal-reminder.png)

Until now, this capability didn’t fully apply to licenses that were [migrated into Freemius](help-documentation-migration.md). We understood this created gaps in recovery for products transitioning to our platform. With today’s update, license recovery support is now fully extended to all relevant migrated licenses.

Whenever a migrated license becomes eligible for recovery, Freemius will send a proper dunning email with a link to Checkout — where customers can not only reinstate their subscription, but also seamlessly “migrate” into Freemius during the process. This ensures you keep revenue continuity while unlocking the full [Freemius platform benefits](freemius-for-saas.md) for those customers.
