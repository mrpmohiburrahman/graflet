[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Improvements to the wire payout methods

As you [sell with Freemius](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius.md), you have various options for your [payouts](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-your-earnings.md), including wire transfers, where the money is sent directly to your bank account.

You can transfer earnings from different currencies to the same or different bank accounts, with the option to convert the earnings to any target currency of your preference.

[![](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/payout-methods-1024x455.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/payout-methods.png)

However, there are certain restrictions on wire transfers that are beyond our control. For example, transferring PKR (Pakistani Rupee) to a PK business account through wire transfer is not supported by regulation.

This week, we are rolling out updates to surface these restrictions directly in the UI as you configure your payout settings. This improvement will help you choose the right currency and avoid delays in receiving your payments. As always, we strive to make the [payout process as efficient and transparent](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-your-earnings.md#is_it_possible_to_expedite_the_payout_this_month) as possible—after all, it’s your money.
