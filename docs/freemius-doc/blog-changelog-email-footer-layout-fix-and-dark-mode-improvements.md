[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Email Footer Layout Fix and Dark Mode Improvements

We identified a regression in last week’s [email style update](blog-changelog-email-system-layout-improvements-and-dark-mode-fixes.md) where the footer was mistakenly pushed inside the main email content area. This unintentionally made the “Sent by Freemius” line more prominent. When sending emails to customers on your behalf, our goal is to give better visibility to your product.

[![Email footer](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/email-footer-1024x513.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/email-footer.png)

With that in mind, we have deployed a fix as shown in the screenshot above. We also ensured the footer renders nicely in dark mode for email clients like Gmail.
