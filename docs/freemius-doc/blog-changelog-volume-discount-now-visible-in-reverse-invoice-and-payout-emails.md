[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Volume Discount Now Visible in Reverse Invoice and Payout Emails

Freemius supports growth pricing or high-volume rates where the more you earn, the more you save. When your monthly gross sales exceed $50,000, the revenue share drops progressively, going as low as 0.5%. You can read more about it in our [docs here](help-documentation-getting-started-our-pricing.md#high-volume-rates).

This discount is applied automatically during the [monthly payout](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-your-earnings.md). With today’s deployment, we are introducing a clearer UI in both the reverse invoice and the payout email so you can easily see the exact volume discount applied to your earnings.

[![Reverse Invoice](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/invoice-volume-discount-1024x781.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/invoice-volume-discount.png)

As you can see, the [reverse invoice](blog-changelog-payouts-email-now-includes-the-reverse-invoice.md) will now prominently display the volume discount applied for the payout.

[![Payout email showing volume discount](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/payout-email-with-volume-discount-1024x681.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/payout-email-with-volume-discount.png)

Similarly, your payout emails will also show the volume discount you received for the month, making it easier to reconcile your earnings at a glance.
