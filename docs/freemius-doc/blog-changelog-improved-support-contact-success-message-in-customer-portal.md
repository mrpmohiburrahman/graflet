[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Improved Support Contact Success Message in Customer Portal

The Customer Portal includes a powerful [support form](help-documentation-users-account-management-support-contact-form.md) that allows your buyers to easily reach out to you. It also supports [upsells](help-documentation-users-account-management-support-contact-form.md#freemius_checkout_integration), [HelpScout](help-documentation-users-account-management-support-contact-form.md#help_scout_docs_integration), and [documentation](help-documentation-users-account-management-support-contact-form.md#public_knowledge_base_integration) integration out-of-the-box. If preferred, you can even link to your [own external support form](help-documentation-users-account-management-support-contact-form.md#link_to_your_own_support_page).

With this week’s deployment, we’ve improved the **success message** shown after a customer submits a support request.

[![Freemius Customer Portal Support Form Message](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/freemius-customer-portal-support-form-message-1024x475.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/freemius-customer-portal-support-form-message.png)

To reduce confusion and improve transparency, the message now clearly displays the product name and the [email address](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-email-settings.md) where the request was sent, helping your customers trust that their message reached the right destination.
