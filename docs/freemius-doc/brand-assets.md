Our brand consists of a simple, geometric but modern wordmark paired with a bold, angular symbol.  
Please use the black logo on bright backgounds and the white logo on dark backgrounds.

Security badges let software makers display a trust signal on their checkout pages, showing buyers that payments are processed securely through Freemius.

Please make sure that the clear space around the logo and logomark is the size of the letter “F”.

The logo should not be used at widths smaller than 100px to maintain clarity and detail.

Our color palette reflects the different states software makers experience throughout their journey. And since everything happens in a digital-first world, every color was carefully tested to meet accessibility standards.
