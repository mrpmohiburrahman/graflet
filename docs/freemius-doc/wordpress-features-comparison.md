### There’s more to selling products online than checkout forms and gateway fees

The day you turn to a product-based business strategy is the day you realize how many third-party products and services you need to serve your plugin/theme to the people who use and love it. The journey to success is complex and has many moving parts that need to work together to stimulate sales and growth. To simplify matters, we’ve broken this journey down into:

![](https://freemius.com/fs-site/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/icon-sowing-your-seed-1.svg)

![](https://freemius.com/fs-site/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/icon-sowing-your-seed-2.svg)

### Sowing Your Seed

![](https://freemius.com/fs-site/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/icon-host-your-digital-product.svg)

Host Your Digital Product

You’re going to need to find a secure hosting service to serve your product from. There’s bad hosting, there’s good hosting, and there’s excellent hosting — it all depends on your budget and priorities.

![](https://freemius.com/fs-site/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/icon-purchase-an-ssl-certificate.svg)

Purchase an SSL Certificate

To ensure your data and transactions are secure, you’ll need to obtain an SSL certificate for your website through a reputable, trusted seller.

![](https://freemius.com/fs-site/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/icon-gateway-fees.svg)

Online sellers have to pay a fee for the services that pass transactional data around the internet to authorize or deny deals. There are a bunch to choose from, so you’ll need to do research until you identify a service that suits your specific needs.

![](https://freemius.com/fs-site/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/icon-gateway-feessoftware-licensing.svg)

To control the number of sites your product is valid for, as well as what happens after a purchased license expires, you’ll need to handle software licensing yourself. This capability usually comes with a price tag.
