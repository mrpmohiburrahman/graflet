[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Improved Formatting in Pro-Forma Invoices

In the beginning of every month ([payout](help-documentation-selling-with-freemius-your-earnings.md) cycle) Freemius sends [automatic emails](blog-changelog-new-feature-monthly-scheduled-payout-email-with-pro-forma-invoice.md) to our makers explaining the upcoming payout along with a copy of a pro-forma invoice.

This week we are rolling out a small but important update to the pro-forma invoice. We identified two issues: in some places the date/time was not properly formatted, and a negative `-` sign was incorrectly added to zero values.

[![Pro-forma invoice formatting fixes](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/proforma-invoice-1006x1024.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/proforma-invoice.png)

Both issues have now been fixed, ensuring your invoices are cleaner and more accurate.
