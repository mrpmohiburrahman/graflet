## Affiliate Platform Improvements: More Control, More Transparency

As we refine the dashboard experience, we’re also rolling out key improvements to the Affiliate Platform to optimize payout management and performance tracking.

**✅ New event callout for pending affiliate balances**

Our [Affiliate Platform](affiliate-platform.md) automatically tracks links and calculates payouts every month. Makers already receive **email notifications** when payouts are due, ensuring timely rewards for affiliates.

Now, we’re enhancing the system with a new webhook: `affiliate.payout.pending`. Each month, the webhook is triggered when payouts are calculated, allowing you to:

- Automate payment flows within your system
- Notify your accounting team for seamless processing

[Learn more here](blog-changelog-new-webhook-get-notified-when-its-time-to-pay-affiliates.md).

[![New Freemius affiliate payout webhook](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/New-Freemius-affiliate-payout-webhook.jpg)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/New-Freemius-affiliate-payout-webhook.jpg)

**✅ Affiliate list export for more flexibility**

As part of our Data Liberation Project, makers can now download their affiliates’ collection directly from their **Developer Dashboards**. The new export feature under the *Affiliate Program → Affiliates* tab means you can easily access and manage this data.

This update caps off our Data Liberation Project, strengthening our commitment to data portability and giving you full control over key business data.
