[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Corrected Product Labeling in Affiliate Application Form

We identified a small bug in our [Customer Portal](help-documentation-users-account-management.md) where, in the [Affiliate Application Form](help-documentation-users-account-management-earn-becoming-an-affiliate.md), every product was incorrectly referred to as a “plugin” regardless of its actual type. This caused confusion, particularly for non-WordPress products like [SaaS and Apps](help-documentation-saas.md).

[![Affiliate Application Form in the Freemius Customer Portal](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/affiliate-form-customer-portal-1024x795.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/affiliate-form-customer-portal.png)

We’ve now fixed this issue so the form correctly reflects the product type. If you’re a maker looking to grow your sales through affiliate marketing, you can [explore how Freemius helps you with a built-in affiliate platform](help-documentation-affiliate-platform.md).
