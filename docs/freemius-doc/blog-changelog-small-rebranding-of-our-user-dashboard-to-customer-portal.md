[Changelog](https://freemius.com/changelog/) / Small Rebranding of our User Dashboard to Customer Portal

In line with our recent [rebranding efforts](blog-freemius-rebrand-website-developer-dashboard.md), we’ve renamed the **User Dashboard** to **Customer Portal**.

The [**Customer Portal**](help-documentation-users-account-management.md) is a hosted solution from Freemius where your buyers (Customers) can log in to manage their subscriptions, access payment history, upgrade plans, retrieve license keys, download products, and more.

[![Freemius Customer Portal](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/freemius-customer-portal-1024x562.png)](https://freemius.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/freemius-customer-portal.png)

It’s completely free and can even be [embedded on your website with SSO](help-documentation-users-account-management-sso-single-sign-on-wordpress.md).

This rebranding is just the first step—more updates are on the way. For now, we’ve updated the logo and refreshed a few outdated phrases. Stay tuned for more exciting changes coming soon!
